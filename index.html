by DILLON C.

The Menagerie Gallery is an unofficial clothing brand that I thought of during quarantine. In search of a progressive lifestyle, I needed a way to express myself and I felt I could do that through producing garments. The goal is to design streetwear products that captures the space that I was mentally in whenever it was designed. Hence the name Menagerie, a menagerie is an exhibit of exotic/wild animals, the brand is my menagerie and every piece put out is an "animal" on exhibit. Each piece being thought of through my thoughts, feelings, and traumas.
